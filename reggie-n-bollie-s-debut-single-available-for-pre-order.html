Reggie 'N' Bollie's Debut Single Available For Pre-Order

September 08, 2024
blog

It’s official, you can now pre-order Reggie ‘N’ Bollie’s debut single ‘New Girl’.

The first single from the 2015 X Factor stars is guaranteed to be a huge club hit and a massive summer anthem.

Since Reggie ‘N’ Bollie came second on the last series of the ITV talent contest, they have signed to Simon Cowell’s record label Syco, recorded their debut album and have toured around the UK building on the fan-base they gained through their time on X Factor.

The lovable due who are originally from Ghana have also recently flown back home to shoot the official music video for ‘New Girl’, and are now gearing up for the official release of the single.

During their time on the show where they were mentored by Geordie babe Cheryl, the boys earned themselves a place in the hearts of the nation as they took the roof off each week with their energetic and fun performances.

Mixing rap, hip-hop, and urban-pop the lads have a wonderful energy about them and always put a smile on people’s faces.

Fans of the rappers have high hopes for their debut single as they hope they can replicate the fun of their X Factor performances into an official release.

‘New Girl’ which is the first single to be lifted from Reggie ‘N’ Bollie’s debut album is available for pre-order now and will be released on May 13th, with the LP set to follow later this year.

You can pre-order the song from itunes and Amazon right now.

Stars including fellow X Factor graduate Fleur East have sent out tweets supporting the release of the guys’ debut single. Fleur tweeted: “@ReggieNBollie wishing you both all the best! Can’t wait to see your video” (sic).
