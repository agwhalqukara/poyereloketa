Derek Jeter Gushes Over Daughter Bella, Reveals Latest Milestones

July 24, 2024

No. 2 is a proud dad! Derek Jeter revealed his daughter Bella’s latest milestones while attending a book launch event for The Stuff in NYC on Monday, May 14.

“She’s crawling. She’s pulling herself up,” he gushed exclusively to Us Weekly. “She’s standing up. She’s going to be walking soon.”

The former New York Yankees captain, 43, and wife Hannah Jeter welcomed their first child together in August 2017. The couple have no concrete Father’s Day plans yet, but they did celebrate Mother’s Day in Miami over the weekend.

“I was at work early. We had a game yesterday, and we went out to dinner,” the part owner of the Miami Marlins told Us. “We spent time with our daughter, put her to sleep and then took her [Hannah] out to dinner.”
