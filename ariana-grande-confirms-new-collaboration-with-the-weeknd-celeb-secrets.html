Save your tears because pop star Ariana Grande just confirmed a new collaboration with fellow icon The Weeknd.

Both stars posted a snippet of a remix version of The Weeknd’s song “Save Your Tears” off his latest album, After Hours, on their social media accounts. Each post features a simple caption that only includes each other’s handles.

https://twitter.com/ArianaGrande/status/1384208507163926536

https://twitter.com/theweeknd/status/1384206199084621833

The “Save Your Tears” remix featuring Grande is expected to release later this week on Friday, April 23. The 27-year-old also cleverly commemorating the third anniversary of her 2018 hit single “no tears left to cry” on her Instagram story.

“Three years ago ‘no tears’” and “this week ‘save your tears’ @theweeknd” the story read, as she shared the post of the snippet.

This remix isn’t the first time the duo have collaborated on a track together. Grande featured The Weeknd on her sophomore album My Everything back in 2014 on her track “Love Me Harder” which peaked at number seven on the Billboard Hot 100 list.

The Weeknd’s solo version of “Save Your Tears” is currently sitting at number five on the Hot 100 chart, and we can only imagine that the remix version featuring Grande will shoot to the top as well.
