Happily ever after! Rob Dyrdek is engaged to his girlfriend Bryiana Noelle Flores — thanks to Disneyland’s Aladdin. The Ridiculousness host pulled off the most elaborate, magical proposal ever on Saturday, April 25.

"I always try to be the best boyfriend I can be. @bryiana_noelle really wanted to go see the Aladdin Show at Disney and I of course made it happen. When the parade of animals rolled through the audience as usual in the Aladdin Show it was her boyfriend atop the elephant and not Prince Ali."

The skateboarding pro, who chronicled their engagement on Instagram, gushed: “I am truly humbled by our love. It is truly a divine creation. @bryiana_noelle you are the love of my life and my true destiny. The day I met you I knew I would spend the rest of my life with you. What our relationship has actually become is million times better then I knew was even possible. My dreams and your dreams are our dreams. I love you more than words could ever describe and can't wait for you to be my wife.” 

After Flores (obviously!) said yes, the happy couple jetted off to the Caribbean. “No better way to celebrate then enjoying the beautiful crystal blue waters of the Caribbean,” Dyrdek captioned a photo of his fiancee’s hand, showing off her insanely massive new diamond ring!

“And now you know, there are 2 sides to every story but this one has the same outcome,” Flores added on her account. "Rob and Bryiana-soon-to-be-Dyrdek shall live happily.. ever.. after.. No end."
